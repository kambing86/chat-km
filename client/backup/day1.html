Q1: How empowered are you?

Write a haiku and win a grand prize

Get creative, inspire your fellow VPs.

Send in a ‘Haiku’ about empowerment. A Haiku is a poem that is developed in syllables/lines of five, seven and five to make up a total of seventeen syllables. Here’s an example:

‘I am decisive (5 syllables)

I can make decisions now (7 syllables)

Empowered I am’ (5 syllables)

Or

I am first with five (5 syllables)

Then seven in the middle (7 syllables)

Five again to end (5 syllables)

Remember, the theme is on EMPOWERMENT. You may submit your entry as an individual, or as a team of up to 5 members.

Submit Haikus from Mon to Thu this week. Voting for your favourite Haikus opens on Fri. Top voted Haiku entry wins SGD250 worth of shopping vouchers.
