The game is simple. Complete the activities, gain points, win attractive prizes, but most of all we want you to be empowered as a champion for DBS! This page is dedicated to help you find your way around and find tips for getting the most out of your experience.

Familiarising yourself

Menu Page:

The Menu Page is where you find out what’s happening and the theme for the day. Activities are sorted by day and clicking on each theme lets you dive right in!

Remember to bookmark this address so you can return with ease: go.dbs.com/champions

What to do once you’re logged in

Complete your activities for the day:

Learn more about the conversation topics through interactive video resources. View the videos and gain points for the day.

Answer the poll questions and let us know what you think! Remember, be honest!

Send in your Haiku:

Get creative and inspire your fellow VPs! Encourage them by voting for the ones you like.

Ask Sim & his SGMC anything:

Always wondered why things are done a certain way? Have an idea that will be the next game changer? Or would you love some career advice from Sim? Send all of them in! They want to hear about what drives you and what you’d like to achieve in DBS. Ask and they will try to answer!

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How can I win the daily prizes?

A: Take part in the quizzes and 20 lucky winners will be selected daily on Tuesday, Wednesday and Thursday (26-28 Jan).


Q: How will I be notified that I’ve won a prize?

A: An email will be sent out daily to announce the winners for the day before.


Q: How can I find out how many points I’ve earned so far?

A: Click on the dropdown menu at the top left of the page. You will see your points in the menu that appears. The more activities that you’ve completed, the more points you earn!
